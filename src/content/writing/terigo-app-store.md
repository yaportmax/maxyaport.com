Terigo is live on the App Store. It is my first app there, which still feels pretty weird to say.

The app started as a route-library tool for myself. I wanted a better way to pull in saved Strava routes, organize them, see them on a map, and stop losing track of the routes I had already saved.

Getting it through App Review took more work than I expected. Screenshots, privacy pages, support pages, review notes, build settings, all the little bits that make it feel like a real thing instead of a project sitting on my laptop.

I am sure I will keep changing it, but version 1.0 is out. That is enough for now.
