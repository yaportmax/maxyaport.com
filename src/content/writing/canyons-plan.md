Before Canyons, I put together a pretty detailed plan for pacing, crew stops, calories, bottles, and the whole race-day food math problem.

It lives here: [Canyons 100K plan](https://yaportmax.github.io/canyons-100k-2026/nutrition.html).

The plan was useful mostly because it forced me to think through the day before I was tired and stupid. I did not follow every single detail, but having the structure made the race feel a lot less mysterious.
